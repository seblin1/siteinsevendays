The Format

How It Works

Everything you love about Survivor. Compressed into one sitting. No jungle required.

01 🔥

The Tribal Phase

Six strangers split into two tribes of three. Compete in challenges. Lose? Head to tribal council and vote someone out. Win? Walk away with advantages and immunity idols hidden across the game.

02 🪬

The Merge

Drop your buffs. Tribal loyalties dissolve and the individual game begins. Everyone is a target. Trust nobody. Every vote counts. Alliances made at the merge rarely survive it.

03 🗳️

Final Tribal Council

The last two players face the jury — everyone they voted out. Make your case. Own your moves. The jury decides who played the best game and crowns the Sole Survivor.

"The tribe has spoken."

— Every tribal council, forever

The Season Arc

Round by Round

Round 1 — Pre-Merge

Fire Making Challenge

Both tribes compete in the fire making challenge. Win immunity and a reward advantage for your tribe. Lose and head straight to your first tribal council.

6 Players Remaining

Round 2 — Pre-Merge

Reward + Immunity Split

Two separate challenges. Win reward to earn a powerful advantage. Win immunity to stay safe. The smaller tribe can't be voted out — but losing carries a disadvantage into the merge.

5 Players Remaining

The Merge

Drop Your Buffs

Tribal phase is over. Every player for themselves. Alliances are tested. Idols become critical. The jury starts forming. One wrong move sends you to Ponderosa.

4–5 Players Remaining

Rounds 3–5 — Individual

Immunity or Go Home

Individual reward and immunity challenges every round. Win immunity and you're safe. Lose and your fate is in the hands of the people you've been lying to for the past hour.

4 → 3 → 2 Players

The Finale

Final Tribal Council

Two finalists. Four jury members. One winner. Make your case for why you deserve the title of Sole Survivor. The jury votes. The torch is raised. Only one name is read.

2 Finalists + 4 Jury

What Makes It Real

Built for Superfans

Every mechanic designed to replicate the show's drama. Not a card game. Not a simulation. The real thing.

🤝

Real Alliances

Private messaging, whispered deals, public promises — and none of it is binding. Trust is a weapon. Betrayal is a strategy. Everything is just words.

🏺

Hidden Immunity Idols

Idols are hidden in the game environment during discussion phase. Find them before anyone else. Hold them. Play them at the right moment. Or get voted out with one in your pocket.

🎯

Dramatic Vote Reveals

Votes revealed one by one. Algorithmically ordered for maximum drama. The decisive vote is always last. Idol plays happen before the votes are read. Exactly like the show.

🧠

Social Challenges

"Most Likely To flip on their alliance?" Everyone points simultaneously. The answers are immediate, personal, and create exactly the paranoia that makes Survivor great.

🗡️

Advantages That Matter

Extra votes, stolen votes, spy abilities, Knowledge Is Power. Some announced publicly. Some completely secret. All transferable. All expiring if you wait too long.

📜

Your Game Story

After every game a shareable summary card captures your season story. "Voted out with an idol in their pocket." "Blindsided 3 players." "Never received a single vote."

"In this game you have to outwit your opponents, outplay them at every turn, and outlast them until only one person remains. That person wins a million dollars and the title of Sole Survivor. But here, the real prize is knowing you played the best game in the room."
